Pizza Engineer

Total Formula

Flour: 1000g
Total Water: 700g
Sea Salt (3%): 30g
Instant Yeast (0.15%): 1.5g

Day 1: Biga Phase

Ingredients: 1000g Flour, 450g Water, 1.5g Yeast.
1. Mix until dry, shaggy clumps form. Do not knead into a ball.
2. Ferment in a sealed container for 16-18 hours at 18°C-20°C.

Day 2: Final Dough Integration

Ingredients: Fermented Biga, 250g Cold Water, 30g Salt.
1. Add biga to mixer. Add roughly half the remaining water (125g). Mix on low for 5 mins.
2. Add salt and mix on medium-low.
3. Bassinnage: Slowly drizzle the final 125g of water over 10 minutes until absorbed.
4. Rest bulk dough for 1 hour at room temp.
5. Divide into portions, shape into tight balls, and proof for 4-6 hours at room temp before baking.
