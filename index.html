Total Formula

Flour: 1000g
Total Water: 700g
Salt (3%): 30g
Yeast: 1.5g

Day 1: Biga Phase

Ingredients: 1000g (Flour), 450g (Water), 1.5g (Yeast).
1. Mix until dry, shaggy clumps form. Do not knead into a ball.
2. Ferment in a sealed container for 16-18 hours at 18°C-20°C.

Day 2: Final Dough Integration

Ingredients: Fermented Biga, 250g (Cold Water), 30g (Salt).
1. Add biga to mixer. Add roughly half the remaining water. Mix on low for 5 mins.
2. Add salt and mix on medium-low.
3. Slowly drizzle remaining water over 10 minutes until absorbed.
4. Rest bulk dough for 1 hour at room temp.
5. Divide into portions, shape tightly, proof 4-6 hours at room temp before baking.
